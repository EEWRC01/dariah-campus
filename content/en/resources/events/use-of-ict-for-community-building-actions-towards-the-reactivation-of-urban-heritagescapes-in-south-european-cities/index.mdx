**[UDigiSH](https://www.dariah.eu/activities/working-groups/digital-practices-for-the-study-of-urban-heritage/)**, the DARIAH Working Group on “Digital Practices for the Study of Urban Heritage”, organised a public space co-creation workshop with the title “Everyday experiences and heritage in southern European cities: Digital tools and practices”, that was held at Cantieri Culturali della Zisa, Palermo, 25-30 July 2021.

This Dariah Campus resource contains the presentations, discussions, and materials made for the workshop.
